The Developer Club was founded in September of 2017 with the objective to promote the digital infrastructure in the Copenhagen International School community. As the club believes technology lies at the heart of well being in today's society, we felt there was a desperate need for technological betterment in our community. The clubs founders wanted to take on that challenge and actively accelerate the digitalization of the CIS ecosystem, through increased interconnectivity, transparency and better UI/UX of its digital platforms.
